\# Next Physical Actions



\## Purpose



This checklist defines the next real-world actions before any wiring starts.



\## Actions



1\. Confirm temporary access to the CODESYS Opta.

2\. Record the exact Opta model/reference.

3\. Confirm 24 V DC PSU model and current rating.

4\. Confirm interface relay coil voltage and contact rating.

5\. Confirm lamp voltage is 24 V DC.

6\. Confirm available terminal blocks and DIN rail.

7\. Confirm available wire, ferrules and labels.

8\. Confirm whether lab has a 24 V load lamp/resistor.

9\. Find or buy two momentary pushbuttons.

10\. Find or buy one maintained 2-position selector switch.

11\. Find or buy fuse holder and suitable fuse.

12\. Find or buy a safe 0-10 V signal source.

13\. Photograph parts before build.

14\. Fill in `parts\_verification\_table.csv`.

15\. Do not wire anything until part ratings are recorded.



\## Safety Rule



No part is considered approved for use until its voltage/current rating is known.

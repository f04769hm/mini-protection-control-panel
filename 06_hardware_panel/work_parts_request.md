\# Work Parts Request



\## Purpose



This document lists the parts requested through proper work/lab channels for the Mini Protection \& Control Panel.



Nothing should be taken without permission.



\## Project Summary



I am building a safe low-voltage 24 V DC training/demo panel to practise protection and control workflow.



The project covers:



\- fault-current calculation

\- IDMT overcurrent settings

\- grading chart

\- software FAT-style testing

\- 24 V trip-circuit demonstration

\- hardwired permissive/blocking

\- relay healthy/watchdog supervision

\- panel wiring documentation



The demonstrator is not connected to mains, real CTs, real breakers or any live installation.

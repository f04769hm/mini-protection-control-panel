\# Parts To Confirm



Before final wiring, confirm the actual part numbers and ratings of the physical devices.



\## Controller



| Item | Confirmed? | Notes |

|---|---|---|

| CODESYS Opta exact model | \[ ] | Record model/reference |

| Opta supply voltage | \[ ] | Confirm 24 V DC supply method |

| Opta input common/reference wiring | \[ ] | Needed before input wiring |

| Opta analogue input scaling | \[ ] | Confirm 0–10 V handling in CODESYS |

| Opta relay output contact ratings | \[ ] | Check suitability for 24 V load/relay circuits |



\## Power



| Item | Confirmed? | Notes |

|---|---|---|

| 24 V DC PSU rating | \[ ] | Current rating must cover Opta, lamps, relays and load |

| Fuse holder rating | \[ ] | Suitable for 24 V DC circuit |

| Fuse size | \[ ] | Choose after load/relay current known |

| Selector switch contact type | \[ ] | Maintained 2-position with suitable contact block |
